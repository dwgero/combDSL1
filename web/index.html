Stepping options

        

            
Single Step

            

                Runs automatically and displays every reduction until normal
                form is reached or Cancel is selected.
            

            
Key Step

            

                Pauses after initial expression scanning. Press any ordinary
                key for exactly one reduction. When normal form is reached,
                the next keypress ends the reduction. Cancel also ends the
                reduction.
            

            
Basis Step

            

                Changes how Single Step and Key Step handle named bases
                defined by compound combinators. All the bird combinators (see
                Combinator Info) except S, K, I, and Y are named bases. Basis
                Step: Off contracts a saturated named basis directly (for
                example, Mx becomes xx). Basis Step:
                On exposes its underlying definition as a separate reduction
                first (so
                Mx becomes SIIx).
            

        

        

            
        

    

    
        
Bird Combinators

        

            Most of the birds come from Raymond Smullyan's book
            To Mock a
                Mockingbird.
            Application is left-associative, meaning xyz at the head of an
            expression or right after a ( acts like ((xy)z).
            Note that the xyz in Cxyz is not at the head; C is.
        

        

            
                

                    Bird
                    Reduction
                

            
            
                
AlbatrossAxy = x(yx)

                
BluebirdBxyz = x(yz)

                
CardinalCxyz = xzy

                
DoveDxyzw = xy(zw)

                
IdentityIx = x

                
KestrelKxy = x

                
LarkLxy = x(yy)

                
MockingbirdMx = xx

                
NightingaleNxy = xyx

                
OwlOxy = y(xy)

                
PeacockPxyz = y(xz)

                
RobinRxyz = yzx

                
SageYx = x(Yx)

                
StarlingSxyz = xz(yz)

                
ThrushTxy = yx

                
VireoVxyz = zxy

                
WarblerWxy = xyy

                
ZazuZxy = x(xy)
