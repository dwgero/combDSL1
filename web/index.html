Step modes

        

            
Single Step

            

                Runs automatically and displays every reduction until normal
                form is reached or Cancel is selected.
            

            
Key Step

            

                Pauses after parsing. Press any ordinary key for exactly one
                reduction. After the last expression is displayed, press one
                more key to confirm normal form. Cancel ends the session.
